## What Is Slay the Spire?

Slay the Spire is a single-player deck-building roguelike developed by Mega Crit. Released in early access in 2017 before its full launch in 2019, the game combines card-game strategy with procedurally generated dungeon exploration.

Players climb a mysterious tower known as "The Spire," battling enemies using customized card decks while collecting relics, upgrading abilities, and making choices that shape each run.

Unlike traditional card games that rely heavily on collecting cards outside gameplay, Slay the Spire builds your deck during each playthrough. Every decision matters, and no two runs are ever exactly alike.
